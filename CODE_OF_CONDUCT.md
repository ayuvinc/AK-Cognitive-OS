# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and inclusive collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and factual.
- Focus feedback on the work, not individuals.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep disagreements technical and specific.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that violates this policy.
